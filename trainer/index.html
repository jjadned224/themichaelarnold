Need a trainer to engage and teach?

Michael is the professional trainer who can provide your team engaging training to develop your team's problem solving and leadership for an overall more effective team.

With his Lean-Six Sigma Master Black Belt, Shanin Red X Journeyman and 25+ years of automotive quality experience, Michael teaches teams the following topics:

Eight Disciplines of Problem Solving

8D training prepares your team to identify underlying root causes of a problem and arms them wtih basic tools to start finding solutions.

Successful Six Sigma Training

For more advanced problem solving training, Michael offers Lean Six Sigma Yellow Belt, Green Belt and Black Belt classes, each is focused on the 20% of tools used 80% of the time. He also offers Champion Training to prepare managers to support their teams as they tackle your organization's biggest problems.

Customer Successes and Feedback: "We've documented $1M in savings from two projects after our team completed Green Belt training with Michael."

Project Leadership

For most of us, teamwork is a part of everyday life. Whether it's at home, in the community, or at work, we are often expected to be a functional part of a project team. Having a strong team will benefit any organization and will lead to more successful projects. This workshop will encourage participants to explore the different aspects of a project team, as well as ways they can become a top-notch team performer.

Adaptive Leadership

Teams are an integral part of any business. It is not enough to have a group of people work on a project; people must connect and balance each other's strengths. Adaptive Leadership training builds on the basic team and self-management strategies covered in Project Leadership Training to help managers and business leaders develop the Top Ten Soft Skills to tackle any situation.
